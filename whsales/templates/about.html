Adarnauth Wormhole Sales

How do I find a wormhole?

The latest listings are available on the front page, newest first. To find a specific system, use the Search form.

How do I buy a wormhole?

If you've found a wormhole you like, get in touch with the character who listed it, found on the listing information page.

Always use a broker. There are many groups who offer brokers, CAPLF Wormhole Sales being the most famous. Adarnof will also do it if he's in-game.

How do I post a wormhole?

Select Add a Listing from the top bar, and log in as the character you want to be visible on the listing. You will then be prompted to enter a system, price and comments.

What powers this site?

The backend is a Django project, used as a testing platform for various technologies making their way into Adarnauth. The website was styled with Bootstrap.

Currently this site is testing adarnauth-eve-sso and being used to prototype adarnauth-acl, adarnauth-singlecharcore and modelowneradmin.

Read about all the apps on the Adarnauth site.

Who profits from this site?

Monetarily? No one. But Adarnof accepts donations.

Technologically? Everyone. The more this site gets used, the more testing that can be done.
