Adarnauth Wormhole Sales

How do I find a wormhole?

The latest listings are available on the front page, newest first. To find a specific system, use the Search form.

How do I buy a wormhole?

If you've found a wormhole you like, get in touch with the character who listed it, found on the listing information page.

Always use a broker. There are many groups who offer brokers, CAPLF Wormhole Sales being the most famous. Adarnof will also do it if he's in-game.

How do I post a wormhole?

Once your character is online in the system you want to sell, log in to the site. Then select Add a Listing from the top bar. You'll be prompted to enter a price and comments.

You may only list the system you are currently in. Your character's location can only be determined while in-game.

I want to list a system that I'm not in!

Too bad. To reduce fraud, I require you prove you're in the system.

Support for adding multiple characters to an account is in development, which will eventually enable the listing of systems you have alts in.

I don't want you to have access to my character's location anymore!

Easy. Simply delete this application from the Third Party Applications section of the EVE Community Site for your character.

What powers this site?

The backend is a Django project, used as a testing platform for various technologies making their way into Adarnauth. The website was styled with Bootstrap.

Currently this site is testing adarnauth-eve-sso and being used to prototype adarnauth-acl, adarnauth-core-singlechar and modelowneradmin.

Read about all the apps on the Adarnauth site.

Who profits from this site?

Monetarily? No one. But Adarnof accepts donations.

Technologically? Everyone. The more this site gets used, the more testing that can be done.

You can take off your tin foil hats, this isn't a conspiracy. That you know of.
